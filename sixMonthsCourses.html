SUMMARY OF SIX-MONTH COURSES

1. First Aid

A comprehensive six-month course covering essential first aid skills, emergency response techniques, and CPR training to equip individuals with the knowledge to handle medical emergencies effectively.

2. Sewing

This six-month sewing course teaches fundamental sewing techniques, pattern making, garment construction, and fabric selection, empowering students to create their own clothing and home textiles.

3. Landscaping

A practical six-month landscaping course focusing on garden design, plant care, soil management, and sustainable landscaping practices to enhance outdoor spaces.

4. Life Skills

This six-month life skills course covers essential topics such as communication, financial literacy, time management, and personal development to help individuals succeed in various aspects of life.
